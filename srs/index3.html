President Volodymyr Zelensky stated that today 700,000 servicemen are defending Ukraine in the war. Source: Zelensky in an interview with Ukrainian journalists Direct speech of the President: "Today it is 700 thousand - here you see the result of the work of 700 thousand people who are fighting. 700 thousand. This is at the time of the war." Source: Details Zelensky added that all this number of people and equipment are scattered throughout the country. According to the president, a country like Ukraine does not need an army of 250,000 or 260,000 (and there were 120,000 combat troops there, Zelensky clarified), but a much larger one. Therefore, in early 2022, he signed a decree to increase the armed forces by 100 thousand next year.At the same time, the president believes that even these plus 100,000 could not stop Russia's full-scale attack.


Ukrainian hackers claim to have hacked into the infrastructure of a network of warehouses in Minsk, which belong to Statio CJSC. They also claim that several websites of Belarusian companies have been hacked. According to the hackers, they hacked the websites obk.by, mcity.by, ortogroup.by, m21.by and globalbel.by. All these sites either do not open, or instead of content, images of the destroyed city of Bucha, information about the war in Ukraine, and an appeal by Volodymyr Zelensky appear.
